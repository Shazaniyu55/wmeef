During Ramadan this year, the Foundation distributed food packages to **300 households** across Warrake and nearby villages.

Each package contained staples to ease the month for families facing hardship: rice, beans, oil, and other essentials. Distribution was carried out by a team of volunteers working alongside community leaders to reach those most in need — including orphans, widows, and elderly residents living alone.

## Made possible by many hands

This effort was funded entirely by community donations and the generosity of local businesses. Volunteers gave their time to pack, sort, and deliver, and community elders helped identify families quietly carrying the heaviest burdens.

## Thank you

To everyone who gave, packed, and delivered — thank you. Welfare is one of our four pillars precisely because no one in our community should face hardship alone.

Plans for next year's drive are already underway. To volunteer or contribute, [contact us](/contact).
