The Foundation awarded **full scholarships to four pupils** at **Attaoheed College of Science and Arabic Studies, Warrake**.

This is the kind of support that sits at the heart of WMEEF. By making Quranic learning the entry point, the Foundation creates an incentive for beneficiaries to pursue both Islamic and conventional education — and removes the cost barriers that too often stand in the way.

## Why scholarships matter

For many families, school fees are the difference between a child continuing their education and stopping short. These scholarships keep four young people learning, growing in character, and grounded in their faith.

We intend for this to be the first of many. To help sponsor a pupil, please [get in touch](/contact).
