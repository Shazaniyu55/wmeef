On **30 May 2026**, the Foundation successfully organised the **1st Warrake Islamic Education Summit** at **Warrake Central Mosque**.

The summit brought stakeholders together around a shared concern: renewing interest in Quranic learning and strengthening moral and Islamic education in the community.

## A gathering with purpose

The aim of the summit was to mobilise the community — families, scholars, and leaders — around Quranic and moral education, and to chart a way forward for Warrake as a hub for Islamic learning once again.

This first summit marks the beginning of an ongoing conversation. We are grateful to everyone who attended, contributed, and lent their support.
