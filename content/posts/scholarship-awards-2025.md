This September, the Foundation welcomed families, teachers, and community elders to celebrate **42 students** receiving scholarship awards for the 2025 academic year.

Each beneficiary received a tuition grant, a full set of textbooks, and a school starter pack. For many of the families present, the support means the difference between a child continuing their education and dropping out.

## Why it matters

Education is the first of our four pillars, and the scholarship fund is its foundation. By covering the costs that most often push children out of school — fees, books, and uniforms — we keep young people learning and give them a real path forward.

> "I want to become a nurse and come back to help my community." — a 2025 awardee

## What comes next

Awardees are paired with a mentor from among past beneficiaries, who check in through the year and help with study habits, subject choices, and confidence. We will share progress updates as the school year unfolds.
