Our first vocational training cohort has graduated. Over four months, **28 participants** trained across three tracks: tailoring, catering, and digital literacy.

The aim of the program is simple: skills that pay. Every graduate left not just with a certificate, but with a starter kit — a sewing machine, catering equipment, or a small stipend toward setting up — so the work can begin immediately.

## The tracks

- **Tailoring** — pattern-making, machine work, and finishing, with a focus on local demand for school uniforms and everyday wear.
- **Catering** — food preparation, hygiene, costing, and small-event service.
- **Digital literacy** — typing, office tools, and the basics of running a small business online.

## Looking ahead

We are now enrolling the second cohort. Priority goes to young people and women who are out of school or out of work. Spaces are limited, and applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
